What is SpongeBob Tower Defense?

SpongeBob Tower Defense is a tower defense game based on the beloved SpongeBob SquarePants cartoon. In this game, players must strategically place defensive towers to protect their base from waves of enemies. Each tower has unique abilities, and players need to choose the right ones to defend against different types of enemies.

The game features various defensive units, each with different attack styles and ranges. For example, the SpongeBob tower can hit multiple enemies within a large radius, while the Patrick tower deals heavy damage to a single enemy. Plan Your Defense Lines Carefully

In SpongeBob Tower Defense, planning your defense line is key to victory. Try to place towers that cover multiple paths and position them where enemies are likely to pass. Continuously adjust your strategy to adapt to new enemy types and wave patterns.
